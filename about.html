How It Started

It Started With a
Forgotten Birthday

A group of friends. A group chat that had gone quiet. And then — the embarrassing realisation that someone's birthday had come and gone, and nobody had said a word.

Not because anyone didn't care. Just because life gets busy, phone contacts don't show birthdays, and no one wants to be the person who has to keep track of thirty different dates.

So we built Birthday Buddy. A shared directory where everyone in a group registers their birthday once — and then never has to worry again. The day arrives, the email goes out, the group lights up. Simple as that.

What We Believe

The Values Behind
Everything We Build

🤝

Community First

Groups thrive when people feel seen. A birthday message is a small thing — but it signals that someone took the time to care. We make that effortless for entire communities.

🎯

Radical Simplicity

No downloads. No accounts required to view. No bloat. One form, one link, and your whole group is connected. We believe the best tools get out of the way.

🔓

Free, Always

Celebrating the people you love shouldn't cost anything. Birthday Buddy is free forever — because connection shouldn't come with a subscription fee.

🔒

Privacy by Default

Your birthday and email are used for exactly one thing: sending birthday reminders. We don't sell data, we don't run ads, and we don't share your info with anyone outside your group.
